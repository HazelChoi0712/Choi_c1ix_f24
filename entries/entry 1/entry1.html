Putitto Kirby Blind Box

This is the one that I have!

I found this figure on the website YAMI and bought it right away!

Price : $6.39 (Not a bad price compared to other figures)

This figure is definitely the star of the package! It is one of those adorable pieces that just begs to be displayed. Seriously, it is *so* cute!! The only tiny letdown is that it is smaller than I expected. Because of that, it does not pop as much next to the other figures in my collection. Still, it is a lovely addition and worth having!

Rate:3.5 out of 5!
